How Staking Rewards Work

Time-Weighted Rewards

Rewards are proportional to amount x duration staked during the epoch. Stake longer, earn more.

30% of Epoch Budget

Stakers share 30% of every epoch's total emission budget. The other 70% goes to partner vaults.

PSRE or LP Tokens

Both PSRE tokens and PSRE/ETH LP tokens from the trading pool are eligible for the same staking pool.

Claim After Epoch

Rewards are calculated at epoch close (every 7 days) and claimable from the staking contract immediately after.
